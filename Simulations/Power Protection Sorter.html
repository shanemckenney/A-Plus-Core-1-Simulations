Power Protection Sorter

Domain 3.6
Devices to Classify
Scenario: A small office experiences brief power flickers a few times per month. Drag each device into the appropriate protection category.

Available Devices

Drag all devices into their protection category → Click Evaluate.
Classification Rationale:
  • UPS: Critical devices where power loss = data/session loss (PC, monitor, modem, router, switch)
  • Surge Protector: Sensitive electronics that don't need battery runtime (scanner, chargers, lamps)
  • Wall Outlet: High-draw/heating devices unsuitable for UPS (especially laser printers)
Protection Categories
UPS battery + surge
Surge Protector surge only
Wall Outlet no protection
