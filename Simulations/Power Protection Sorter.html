Rationale (teach-back):
  • UPS: devices where sudden power loss causes session/data loss or takes down critical connectivity (PC, monitor, modem/router/switch).
  • Surge: sensitive electronics that don’t need battery runtime (scanner, chargers, lamps, speakers).
  • Wall: high-draw/heating devices that don’t belong on a UPS (especially laser printers).
